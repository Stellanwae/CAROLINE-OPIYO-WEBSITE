About Caroline

Redefining Urban Sustainability

With a rare combination of environmental planning expertise and full-stack development skills, I bring a unique perspective to urban sustainability challenges. My work sits at the intersection of ecological preservation and technological innovation.

Having collaborated with government agencies, NGOs, and private sector partners across Kenya, I've developed a holistic approach to urban development that balances growth with environmental stewardship.

My mission is to leverage technology to make environmental planning more accessible, data-driven, and impactful - creating cities that are not just sustainable, but regenerative.
